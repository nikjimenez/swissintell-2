Our Journey

Two decades building
the Swiss intelligence community

2006 · Founding

Swissintell founded in Lausanne

A handful of intelligence professionals set up the first Swiss nonprofit dedicated to strategic & competitive intelligence.

2013 · Publications

First members-only archive

Launch of the private publications programme — today a library of 650+ briefings and analyses.

2019 · JVeille

Annual Intelligence Festival

JVeille becomes the reference annual gathering, drawing 300+ attendees to EPFL each spring.

2016 · Expansion

EN · FR · DE programme

Activities extended across the three national languages, reaching professionals in Bern, Geneva and Zurich.

2022 · Verticals

8 thematic intelligence verticals

Dedicated streams on Cyber, Geopolitics, AI, Industry 4.0, Defense, Economic Intel, Data Protection, Competitive Intel.
